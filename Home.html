About Us


Work of Love Cafe is a coffee shop owned by Lourdes Angelie Viray. It is located in Triax Two Center San Guillermo St., Buting, Pasig City, and it is open from 11:00 AM to 10:00 PM, but on Fridays and Saturdays, the shop is open until 11:00 PM. Moreover, Work of Love Cafe serves various drinks like coffee, tea, milkshakes, lemonade, latte, and some classics like root beer float and Irish coffee. Aside from this, Work of Love cafe also serves quality and delicious food to customers, which can vary, like all-day breakfast menus and starter menus, sandwiches, and different flavors of pasta.
